Volunteering in High School Clubs

Benefits of Volunteering in High School Clubs

  • It looks good for college! Colleges love to see you give back to your community and volunteer
  • It's a great way to meet new friends, school is where most people meet lifelong friends
  • It teaches responsibility to prepare you for the real world, your contributions and efforts when volunteering not only impact you but everyone that is involved and affected by the project you are apart of
  • It allows you to learn new skills, depending on the type of volunteering you sign up for

Expectations When Volunteering

  • Be respectful! You are representing your club and ultimately your school when you volunteer
  • Listen to other volunteers and follow instructions
  • Be ready to work, it is important you give your best effort when you volunteer
  • Be prepared for the unexpected, you may have to change tasks or improvise due to weather when volunteering

Clubs to Join/Create in High School

  • National Honors Society
  • Habitat for Humanity
  • Key Club
  • Interact
  • Tutoring Club
  • Operation Smile Club
  • Breast Cancer Awareness
  • There are a lot more! These are just a few ideas. Take some time to think about what matters to you!

How to Create a Club

  • Find a meaningful club to create.
  • Search the subject of the club online for more information on what people around the world are doing with the subject.
  • Find a sponsor at school. Choose a teacher who you are close with or who you think would be interested in the subject of your club.
  • Email that teacher! You can then follow your school guidelines from here!
