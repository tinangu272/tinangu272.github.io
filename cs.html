Community Service

Benefits of Community Service

  • Take an active role in your community.
  • Will redefine or empower your morals.
  • Learn to be responsible for yourself and others.
  • Help you learn valuable life skills.

Expectations When Volunteering

  • Comply with the programs policies.
  • Will be working with people of all ages.
  • Might need training for role.
  • Be punctual, reliable, and responsible.
