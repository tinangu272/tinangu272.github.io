Volunteering in Support of Human Rights

Benefits of Volunteering for Human Rights

  • Exemplifies your support and commitment to human rights.
  • It promotes the equal treatment to everyone regardless of religion, sexuality, color, etc.
  • Learn more about different cultures when you get to meet unique people.
  • Help create a positive change for many people's futures.

Expectations When Volunteering

  • Be ready to assist in different activities such as events, lobby greetings, etc.
  • Treat everyone with respect.
  • Be dependable, show up on time and follow through with your commitments.
  • Be able to communicate effectively.
