Volunteering For the Environment

Benefits of Volunteering for the Environment

  • Volunteer work experience will impress future employers by showing you are committed to a cause or belief that you are interested or passionate about.
  • You will become more aware of the many problems the environment faces and be more proactive in your daily life.
  • Give back to the environment and help preserve our planet and eco-system.
  • Inspire others to be more involved in helping and caring about the environment.

Expectations When Volunteering

  • Be willing to get dirty and/or wet
  • Be willing to listen, not following directions can impact the project negatively and cause problems
  • Be flexible, the weather may cause a change in plans
  • Dress appropriately depending on the guidelines of where you volunteer at Dress appropriately depending on the guidelines of where you volunteer at
