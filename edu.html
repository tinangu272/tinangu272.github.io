Volunteering To Educate Our Youth

Benefits of Volunteering with Youth

  • It teaches you how to work with children
  • It'll make a direct impact, childhood is when kids grow and develop their character
  • It helps give a sense of purpose
  • Children are our future, by volunteering with them you are making a better tomorrow

Expectations When Volunteering

  • Set a good example for the children you volunteer with.
  • Be patient and willing to help them if they ask.
  • Have some communication skills if you want to volunteer, it is an important skill when working with children.
  • Be willing to contribute your time and best efforts.
