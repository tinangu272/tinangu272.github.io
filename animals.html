Volunteering For Animals

Benefits of Volunteering for Animals

  • By volunteering with animals at a shelter, they are more likely to be adopted! Animals that enter a shelter typically have little to no human interaction and by working with the animals, you can build their trust for humans, making them more desirable for adoption.
  • Spending time with animals lowers your stress level and blood pressure!
  • It is a great way to meet new people and spend time with animals if you cannot adopt.
  • It offers a good hands-on experience and opportunity to learn new skills.

Expectations When Volunteering

  • Be able to commit a certain amount of hours each month.
  • Walk dogs and socialize cats.
  • Clean cages and other maintenance.
  • Feeding, watering, and grooming.
