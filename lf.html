Volunteering For the Less Fortunate

Benefits of Volunteering for the Less Fortunate

  • It is rewarding to help others that do not have the same privleges as you.
  • Learn from others' perspectives and walks of life.
  • It helps give you a sense of purpose.
  • It improves your mental health.

Expectations When Volunteering

  • Treat people with dignity and respect, and understand that they may be going through a very difficult time in their lives.
  • Be dependable and contribute to the project you are apart of.
  • Be willing to listen and help if anyone asks.
  • Dress appropriately depending on the guidelines of where you volunteer at.
